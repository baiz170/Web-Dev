Fluffy Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1 cup
Milk 1 cup
Egg 1
Baking powder 2 tsp

Step 1: Mix flour, baking powder, milk, and egg into a smooth batter.

Step 2: Heat a lightly oiled pan over medium heat. Pour batter and cook until golden brown.

Step 3: Serve warm with syrup or fruit.

Source: AllRecipes

Spaghetti Carbonara

  • Time: 25 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 200 g
Bacon 100 g
Eggs 2
Parmesan cheese 50 g

Step 1: Cook spaghetti until al dente.

Step 2: Fry bacon until crispy. Mix eggs and Parmesan in a bowl.

Step 3: Combine hot pasta with egg mixture and bacon. Serve immediately.

Source: Simply Recipes

Grilled Salmon

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Salmon fillet 2 pieces
Lemon 1
Olive oil 2 tbsp
Herbs (thyme/rosemary) 1 tsp

Step 1: Preheat grill to medium heat. Brush salmon with olive oil and herbs.

Step 2: Grill salmon for 5-7 minutes per side until cooked through.

Step 3: Serve with lemon slices and a side salad.

Source: BBC Good Food
